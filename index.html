ANU Nanopore Workshop
June 14-16, 2017

Abstract

The ANU Research School of Biology MinION Users Group is hosting a multi-day workshop
to gain experience working with the Oxford Nanopore sequencing technology.

The workshop is tailored to give new users hands-on experience sequencing on the MinION
platform as well as the basics of data analysis to obtain sequence data.

See other workshop examples such as the original UK PoreCamp and porecamp AU.
